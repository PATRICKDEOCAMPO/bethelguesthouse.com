About Us

BETHEL GUEST HOUSE

Bethel Guest House a five storey hotel is named after the biblical "Place of God" provides guests with an atmosphere that allows them to commune with God and be close to nature. Making it a home away from home and your best convention hotel in Dumaguete. Bethel Guest House started its business operations last November 18, 1996. The strong Christian values of the owners give them the resolve to uphold a "No smoking and No Liquor policy" daring to be different from the rest of the players in the hotel industry.
